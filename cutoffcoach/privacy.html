Privacy Policy — CutoffCoach

Last updated: June 7, 2026

CutoffCoach ("the App") helps you log your caffeine intake and predicts a recommended sleep cutoff time. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles and how.

In short: All of your personal data is stored only on your device. The App has no accounts, no sign-up, and does not transmit your personal data to any server.

1. Data We Store on Your Device

To provide its features, the App stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Body weight
  • Caffeine sensitivity setting
  • Target bedtime
  • Caffeine intake records

This data never leaves your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store it on any server, and there is no account or registration. If you delete the App, this data is removed with it.

2. Advertising

The App displays banner advertisements through Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google AdMob may collect and process information such as advertising identifiers and device information.

On iOS, the App asks for your permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT):

  • If you allow tracking, you may receive personalized ads.
  • If you decline, the App requests non-personalized ads (NPA) only.

For details on how Google handles data when you use its partners' sites or apps, see Google's "How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services" policy.

3. Health Disclaimer

CutoffCoach is provided for informational purposes only. Its predictions and recommendations are not medical advice and should not be used as a substitute for professional medical guidance. Consult a qualified healthcare provider for any health-related decisions.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
